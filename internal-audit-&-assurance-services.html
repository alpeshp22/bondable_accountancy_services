Internal Audit & Assurance Services

Financial Audit

A financial audit focuses on an organization's financial statements to ensure accuracy and compliance with accounting standards and regulatory requirements. It also verifies the organization's financial position and transactions.

Compliance Audit

Compliance audits ensure that an organization adheres to relevant laws, regulations, industry standards, and internal policies. These audits help mitigate legal and regulatory risks.

Vendor and Supplier Audit

Organizations often wants to conduct audits of their vendors and suppliers to assess performance, compliance, and contractual obligations.

Project Audit

Project audits evaluate the progress, performance, and outcomes of specific projects to identify areas for improvement and ensure project objectives are met.

Risk Assessment and Management

Risk assessment services help organizations identify, assess, and manage risks across various aspects of their operations, including financial, operational, strategic, and compliance risks.
